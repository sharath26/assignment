SUPPORTING OUR COMMUNITIES

Incyte Involved includes the following three initiatives focused on philanthropy as well as employee and community engagement.

THE INCYTE CHARITABLE GIVING FOUNDATION

The Incyte Charitable Giving Foundation provides charitable donations to publicly funded 501(c) (3) tax-exempt nonprofit organizations that are specifically serving the needs of communities in Delaware and are focused on two areas—Oncology Patient Support and Resources and Community Partnerships.

Oncology Patient Support and Resources

The Incyte Charitable Giving Foundation will provide general operating or program support to organizations that offer services or resources to help oncology patients and their families in Delaware cope with their disease/condition.

Application Process
  • All requests must be submitted by the requesting organization using the Incyte Charitable Giving Foundation online application. Please click here to access the application.
  • A completed and signed W-9 form (organization name and address must match funding request form) is required to be submitted with all applications
  • Organizations are required to submit their most recent IRS Form 990 with all applications
  • Inquiries regarding the status of an application can be sent to foundation@incyte.com

Click here to View Funding guidelines

Important Information for All Applicants
  • Requests will only be accepted based on the predetermined quarterly submissions calendar
  • Any contribution should be considered a one-time commitment without guarantee of renewal unless a multiple year commitment is specifically agreed to in writing by the Incyte Charitable Giving Foundation
  • No more than 25% of an organization’s total funding for the year may come from Incyte Corporation and the Incyte Charitable Giving Foundation

The Incyte Charitable Giving Foundation has the final determination as to which requests it will fund.

COMMUNITY SERVICE PROGRAM

Incyte’s Community Service Program provides employees with paid time off each year to volunteer at a non-profit organization of their choice.

MATCHING GIFTS PROGRAM

Incyte’s Matching Gifts Program is a year-round initiative that supports employees’ philanthropic efforts and helps them achieve their charitable giving goals by facilitating financial contributions to eligible charitable organizations. Contributions to eligible non-profit organizations will be matched by Incyte Involved on a dollar-for-dollar basis, up to a predetermined cap.
